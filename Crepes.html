About

Crepes are special. Mom used to make them all the time when we were kids. They look fancy, but really aren't that hard to make. This entire recipe is made in a blender. Also, you can make these savory or sweet. Our family likes them for breakfast with jelly, jam, or Nutella indide!

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour

1 tsp salt

2 tsp baking powder

4 tbsp powdered sugar

4 eggs

2 cup soy or almond milk

A glug of vanilla or amaretto

  • Step-1

    Add eggs, milk, and vanilla/amaretto to a blender.

    Add salt, baking powder and powdered sugar then blend.

    Add flour a ½ c. at a time, pulsing the blender between additions

  • Step-2

    Heat up a nonstick pan. When the pan is hot, take a frozen stick of butter and “draw” a slick of butter around the edge of the pan. If your butter turns brown right away, it’s too hot.

  • Step-3

    Pour batter into the center of the hot pan until it covers about half the area. S wirl the pan until the batter covers the base of the pan. Cook until the top is dry. Flip the crepe and cook for another minute or so.

  • Step-4

    Fill with the fillings of your choice, and enjoy!

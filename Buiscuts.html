About

This recipe is from Salt Springs Brewery, a restaurant the both Ben and Ava have worked at. Biscuits are just good, and are so vercitial. You can eat them with savory things such as chilli, or with a little bit of jam for dessert. While not a very technical recipe, it might take a couple of tries to get these perfect. However, they are worth every little bit of effort!

Ingredients

2 pounds refrigerated butter

6 cups buttermilk

1.4kg All Purpose Flour

740g pastry flour

½ cup baking powder

2 tsp baking soda

½ cup sugar

¼ cup salt

  • Step-1

    Preheat your oven to 350°F

    In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt.

  • Step-2

    Cut refrigerated butter into ½ inch cubes and add to dry ingredients.

    Mix butter and dry ingredients until all the butter is coated.

  • Step-3

    One quarter cup at a time, mix in buttermilk with hands until combined.

  • Step-4

    On a clean work surface sprinkled with flour, turn out the dough.

  • Step-5

    Sprinkle flour on the top of the dough then roll out dough and fold in half seven times sprinkling flour with each fold.

  • Step-5

    Use a biscuit cutter without twisting.

    Use scraps once to make more biscuits!

  • Step-6

    Place biscuits in the oven for 15-20 minutes

  • Step-7

    Enjoy! Great with spicy chilli or a little dizzel of honey.
